Two pillars of enhanced photoemission
E. M. Purcell (1912 - 1997)
 Nobel Prize in 1952
Prof. Klaus Petermann
 Technische Universität Berlin
  • Purcell factor: spontaneous emission enhancement due to small mode volume
  • Petermann factor: excessive spontaneous emission due to astigmatism (non-orthogonality of modes)
  • Both can enhance photoemission greatly.
